Anomaly Detection is the problem of finding patterns in data that do not conform to a model of “normal” behavior. Detecting such deviations from expected behavior in temporal data is important for ensuring the normal operations of systems across multiple domains such as economics, biology, computing, finance, ecology and more. Applications in such domains need the ability to detect abnormal behavior which can be an indication of systems failure or malicious activities, and they need to be able to trigger the appropriate steps towards taking corrective actions. In each case, it is important to characterize what is normal, what is deviant or anomalous and how significant is the anomaly.
